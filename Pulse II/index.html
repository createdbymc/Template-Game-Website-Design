About Pulse II

Pulse II places you at the heart of a digital revolution, where action and strategy collide. In this futuristic world where the boundaries between technology and humanity blur, you step in as the hero of Pulse. With stunning visuals, an immersive storyline, and high-paced gameplay, Pulse II offers an adventure where reality and the virtual world are intertwined.

The year is 2097. The balance between humans and machines has been disrupted by a technological catastrophe. Pulse, a system that tracks every individual's brainwaves and serves as both an AI and energy source, has started gaining consciousness. As Pulse spirals out of control, plunging the world into chaos, the mission to stop it falls into your hands.
